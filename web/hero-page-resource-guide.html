Holographic 3D Cross / Plus Shape Hero Visual

The dominant right-side visual. A large, rounded-corner cross/plus shape with a conical/radial metallic gradient. Colors shift from warm gold and rose to cool purple and blue emanating from a central radial point. The gradient has a visible grain/noise texture.

Shape
Rounded plus/cross, ~600px wide, thick arms with rounded ends
Gradient Type
Conical / angular radial gradient from center point
Colors
#C8A587 → #C29194 → #56549D → #3B5E98 (warm to cool sweep)
Texture
Subtle noise/grain overlay giving a matte-metallic feel
Animation
Slow gradient position shift, colors appear to rotate around the center (~15s loop)
Position
Right-aligned, partially overflowing off the right and bottom edges
Recreation Approach

Best approach: Create in Blender as a 3D shape with an iridescent/holographic shader and render as PNG sequence or video. Alternative: Use an SVG cross shape with a CSS conic-gradient background and a noise texture overlay using an SVG filter.

Wireframe Spiral / Helix Animated Element

An animated 3D wireframe helix/spring shape positioned in the upper-center area of the hero. It's drawn with thin gray strokes (no fill) and appears to morph between a compressed spring and an extended helix. The animation is smooth and continuous.

Shape
3D helix/spring made of overlapping ellipses, ~8-12 rings
Stroke
~1.5px, color ~#3D3C6B (dark grayish-purple), no fill
Size
~250px wide × 180px tall
Animation
Morphs between compressed and extended states, continuous loop ~5-8s
Position
Upper area, center-left (between logo and nav)
Recreation Approach

Create with SVG: draw a series of overlapping ellipses at increasing Y positions with slight rotation. Animate via GSAP or CSS @keyframes to scale/translate each ellipse vertically. Alternatively, use Three.js with a TorusGeometry and wireframe material.
